## Source
Instagram: @manafishrov / @ayaan_raj_khanna

## Summary
- Fully 3D-printed 3-inch FPV unibody frame.
- Uses generative/topology optimization.
- Designed for 100% infill printing.
- Claimed filament cost about $1 per frame.
- Current status: Pre-Alpha 6.0.
